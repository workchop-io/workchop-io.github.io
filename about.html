About the studio

A workshop for our own ideas.

Workchop is a small team that builds its own apps. The name kind of says it all: we take big, intimidating ideas and chop them into pieces small enough to actually ship, with AI as our sharpest tool.

Our story

We'd rather build than bill.

We didn't want to build other people's roadmaps. We wanted to build our own.

So Workchop became a product studio. A place to chase the apps we wish existed and put them out into the world. No client briefs, no billable hours. Just our own ideas, shipped.

AI is what makes this possible at our size. It handles the repetitive bits at every step, from design and code to content and testing, so a small team can launch and look after a whole family of apps. That's the chop.
