Privacy policy

Plain and simple: we only collect what we need to reply to you or send you updates you asked for, and we never sell it.

Last updated: 21 June 2026

Who we are

Workchop is a small studio that builds its own apps. This policy covers our website at workchop.io. If you have any questions, email us at hello@workchop.io.

What we collect, and why

  • Contact form. When you message us, we collect your name, email address, the topic you pick, and whatever you write. We use this only to read and reply to your message. Submissions are delivered to our inbox by Web3Forms.
  • Newsletter. If you subscribe, we collect your email address so we can send you occasional updates about our apps. Our list is managed by Loops.

We do not use advertising or tracking cookies on this site. Our pages load fonts from Google Fonts, which means your browser contacts Google to fetch them, and that request includes your IP address.

Who we share it with

We never sell your information. We only share it with the services that help us run the site:

  • Web3Forms processes and delivers contact form submissions.
  • Loops stores newsletter subscribers and sends our emails.
  • GitHub Pages hosts and serves the website.
  • Google Fonts serves the typefaces the site uses.

How long we keep it

We keep contact messages for as long as we need them to help you. We keep newsletter subscriptions until you unsubscribe.

Your choices

  • You can unsubscribe from the newsletter at any time using the link in the footer of any email we send.
  • You can ask us to show you, correct, or delete the information we hold about you by emailing hello@workchop.io.

Children

This site is not intended for children under 16, and we do not knowingly collect their information.

Changes to this policy

If we change how we handle your information, we will update this page and the date at the top.

Contact

Questions about your privacy? Reach us any time at hello@workchop.io.
